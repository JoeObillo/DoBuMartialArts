JUDO.


Judo is a Japanese martial art and combat sport that focuses on close-quarters grappling, throws, and takedowns. It is practiced by millions of people around the world.

KARATE.


Karate is a Japanese martial art that focuses on striking techniques, such as punching, kicking, knee and elbow strikes, and open-handed techniques such as knife-hands (karate chop).

JIU-JITSU.


Jiu-jitsu is a Japanese martial art that focuses on close-quarter grappling and submission holds. Its practitioners has to force their opponents to submission.

MUAY-THAI.


Muay Thai, also known as Thai boxing, is a combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "Art of eight limbs",
